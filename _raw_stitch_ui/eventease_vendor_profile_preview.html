About Us

Nestled in the rolling hills just outside San Francisco, Rosewood Gardens offers a breathtaking blend of rustic charm and modern elegance. Our historic estate, established in 1924, features over 15 acres of manicured botanical gardens, ancient oak groves, and a newly renovated glass conservatory perfect for dancing under the stars.

Whether you dream of an intimate garden ceremony or a grand reception, our dedicated team of planners ensures every detail is executed flawlessly. We pride ourselves on our farm-to-table culinary experiences, sourcing ingredients directly from our on-site organic garden and local partners.
