Vendor Terms of Service

Last updated: October 24, 2023

Welcome to the EventEase Vendor community. These terms govern the relationship between EventEase ("Platform") and the service providers, merchants, and vendors ("Vendor") listing services on our marketplace.

1

Vendor Eligibility & Verification

To qualify as a Vendor on EventEase, you must represent a legitimate business entity capable of entering into binding contracts. By signing up, you agree to undergo our mandatory verification process.

  • Business Documentation: You must provide valid tax identification and business registration documents upon request.
  • Insurance Requirements: Vendors operating in physical spaces (e.g., Venues, Caterers) must maintain active general liability insurance with coverage of at least $1,000,000.
  • Quality Standards: EventEase reserves the right to audit vendor profiles and past event performance reviews to ensure platform quality.
2

Commission & Payment Structure

Our platform operates on a performance-based commission model designed to align our success with yours.

Standard Commission

A flat 12% commission applies to all bookings secured through the EventEase direct booking tool.

Payout Schedule

Funds are released to your connected bank account 48 hours after the successful completion of the booked event.

Note: Processing fees (approx 2.9% + $0.30) charged by our payment processor (Stripe) are deducted separately from the total transaction amount.

3

Content & Intellectual Property

You retain all rights to the photos, videos, and descriptions you upload to your EventEase profile. However, you grant us a license to use these materials for marketing the Platform.

Prohibited Content: You may not upload content that is misleading, offensive, or infringes on the copyright of others. We employ automated tools to detect stock imagery fraud.

4

Cancellation & Dispute Resolution

Vendors must clearly state their cancellation policies on their listing. In the event of a dispute:

  1. Users and Vendors are encouraged to resolve issues directly through the Message Center.
  2. If a resolution cannot be reached, EventEase will mediate based on the documented cancellation policy and evidence provided.
  3. Decisions made by the EventEase Trust & Safety team are final regarding platform refunds.
